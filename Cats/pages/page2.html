GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest land animals in the world. They can grow up to 18 feet tall and live in Africa. They usually use their long necks to eat leaves from tall trees. Even though Giraffes are very tall, they are gentle animals with strong legs and unique spotted coats.
